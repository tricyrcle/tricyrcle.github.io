How To Set Up Cantina VPN

  • K G
  • Thursday, May 13, 2021

Quick start guide of CantinaVPN service

1: Download from Google Play or Apple iOS app stores

  • Google Play
  • Apple App Store

2: Open the app

Click “browse menu” to select from our tasty set of services. Select the one you’d like to try.

3 : Check out each page

Navigation footer options

  • Cantina VPN (maracas)
  • Menu (lima)
  • Your Order (sombrero)
  • About (muerto)

4 : Cantina VPN

ON. Maracas are shaking.

  • Time or bandwidth remaining is shown.
  • You can select from a list of possible exit countries.
  • A small “key” is shown on the smartphone top navigation bar.

OFF. Maracas stop shaking.

  • Your traffic is not being routed through the VPN.
  • No “key” is shown on the smartphone top navigation bar.

5 : Menu

Select one of the menu items.

  • Optimize speed with affordable anonymity => Snack Ticket
  • Speed plus best value bandwidth that keeps you invisible => Meal Deal
  • Try a Beta Bite for free!

6 : Your Order

This page shows the status of your current order, and the amount of time or bandwidth remaining. If no order is shown, you can input a receipt purchased from the menu or received elsewhere.
